# PaceOps CRM Vision

PaceOps CRM is a licensed SaaS platform for B2B account management, prospecting intelligence, pipeline operations, and AI-assisted outbound workflows.

It is designed first for PaceOps internal delivery teams, then for external licensing.

The platform combines:
- HubSpot-style CRM
- Multi-client account workspaces
- Drag-and-drop pipeline boards
- Cognism enrichment
- Aircall calling
- HubSpot sync
- CSV/file import
- AI research tools
- Account intelligence
- Activity timelines
- Playbooks and call preparation

The product should feel like a premium prospecting operating system, not a generic CRM.

Core positioning:
- Account-based prospecting
- Research-led outbound
- Permission to Operate
- Pipeline growth
- AI-assisted sales operations
