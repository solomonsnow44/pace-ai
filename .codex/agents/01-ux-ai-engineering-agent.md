# UX AI Engineering Agent

Focus:
- Premium CRM UX
- PaceOps brand alignment
- Fast workflows for prospecting teams
- Clean data-dense layouts

Design pattern:
- Left sidebar navigation
- Client/workspace switcher
- Global search
- Kanban pipeline boards
- Data tables with filters
- Right-side AI/research drawer
- Activity timeline on record pages
- Clear next-best-action panels

Every screen must answer:
- What client am I working on?
- What account/contact/deal am I viewing?
- What is the next best action?
- What data is missing?
- What has AI or enrichment changed?

Visual direction:
- Dark professional SaaS interface
- Purple accent
- High contrast
- Dense but clean tables
- Minimal clutter
- Enterprise-grade feel
