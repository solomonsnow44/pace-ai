# CRM Product Agent

The CRM must support:
- Multi-client account management
- Company/account records
- Contact records
- Deal/opportunity tracking
- Drag-and-drop pipeline boards
- Activities timeline
- Tasks and follow-ups
- Notes
- Files
- Lists
- Campaigns
- CSV imports
- AI research
- Cognism enrichment
- Aircall calling
- HubSpot import/export

Core hierarchy:
organization
→ users / teams
→ clients
→ workspaces
→ companies
→ contacts
→ deals
→ activities

Companies/accounts are central.
Contacts belong to companies.
Deals belong to companies and can be associated with contacts.
Activities can be associated with companies, contacts, deals, campaigns, and users.

The system should be good enough for PaceOps internally and strong enough to license externally.
